Shakshuka

Description

Traditional Tunisian dish featuring poached eggs in a spicy tomato sauce with onions, bell pepper, and garlic.

It's usually eaten for breakfast but can make a tasty lunch or dinner.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 1/3 cups chopped onion
  • 1 cup thinly sliced bell peppers
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced, or to taste
  • 2 1/2 cups chopped tomatoes
  • 1 hot chile pepper, seeded and finely chopped, or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 large eggs
